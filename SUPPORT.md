# Support & Contact Policy

## Channels

| Purpose | Channel | Response target |
|---|---|---|
| Business / hiring inquiries | nadi@nadidoug.com | 1–2 business days |
| Bug reports | GitHub Issues (per project) | Best effort, 7 days |
| Security vulnerabilities | See [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) | 72 hours |
| Consulting (SITSOL) | nadi@nadidoug.com / nadidoug.com | 1–2 business days |

## Scope of Support

Portfolio projects are provided as-is for demonstration. They are **not supported products** — no SLA, no guaranteed fixes. Commercial support for SITSOL client work is governed by the applicable client agreement, not this policy.

## What to Include

For technical issues: project name, environment (OS, runtime version), steps to reproduce, expected vs. actual behavior.

## Ground Rules

Support communication is subject to the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Abusive or spam messages will not receive a response.
