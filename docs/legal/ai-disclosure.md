# AI Disclosure

**Last updated:** July 2, 2026

Transparency about where and how artificial intelligence is used across this portfolio and SITSOL projects.

## 1. AI in Development

Some code in this portfolio was developed with AI coding assistants (e.g., Claude, GitHub Copilot). All AI-assisted code is **reviewed, tested, and understood by me** before inclusion. Architecture, design decisions, and final implementations are my own.

## 2. AI Features in Projects

Projects with AI-powered functionality (chatbots, content generation, lead-qualification engines) are identified in their READMEs, including:

- **Provider/model** used (e.g., Anthropic Claude, self-hosted models)
- **What data is sent** to the AI provider and that provider's retention behavior
- **Human oversight** — whether outputs are reviewed before reaching end users

## 3. AI-Generated Content

Where site copy, images, or marketing material is substantially AI-generated, it is labeled `[AI-assisted]`. Portfolio project descriptions and case studies are human-written.

## 4. Limitations Notice

AI outputs can be inaccurate, biased, or outdated. AI features in demos are for evaluation only and must not be relied on for medical, legal, financial, or other consequential decisions.

## 5. Your Data and AI

Do not submit confidential or sensitive personal information to AI demos. Inputs may be processed by third-party providers under their terms. See the [Privacy Policy](privacy.md).
