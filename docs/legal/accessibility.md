# Accessibility Statement

**Last updated:** July 2, 2026

Nadir Douglas / SITSOL is committed to making nadidoug.com and hosted projects accessible to the widest possible audience.

## Conformance Target

**WCAG 2.2, Level AA** is the target standard for the main portfolio site. Individual experimental demos (e.g., WebGL/3D experiences) may not fully conform; where so, an accessible alternative or description is provided.

## Measures Taken

- Semantic HTML and correct landmark/heading structure
- Keyboard navigability and visible focus indicators (WCAG 2.4.11–2.4.13)
- Color contrast meeting AA ratios, verified against the site palette
- Alt text for informative images; decorative images marked appropriately
- `prefers-reduced-motion` respected in scroll-driven and animated experiences
- Form labels, error identification, and target sizes per WCAG 2.5.8

## Known Limitations

- [3D product viewers] rely on pointer interaction; a keyboard/list fallback is [provided / planned].
- Third-party embeds may not meet AA; alternatives available on request.

## Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, email **nadi@nadidoug.com** with the page URL and issue. Target response: 3 business days. This statement is reviewed annually.
